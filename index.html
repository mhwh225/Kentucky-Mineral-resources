The distribution of Kentucky’s Mineral resources.

According to USGS.gov "MRDS is a collection of reports describing metallic and nonmetallic mineral resources throughout the world. Included are deposit name, location, commodity, deposit description, geologic characteristics, production, reserves, resources, and references. It subsumes the original MRDS and MAS/MILS. MRDS is large, complex, and somewhat problematic. This service provides a subset of the database comprised of those data fields deemed most useful and which most frequently contain some information, but full reports of most records are available as well."

This map shows off some of the most common data for kentucky, You can click the point on the map to see some basic infomation like site name, production status, and main commodity. You can aslo use the dropdown menu in the top right of the map to narrow down the info you want to see.
